Here are some of the mods I made for Kerbal Space Program.

Kerbal Space Program, abbreviated to KSP, is a physics-based, space flight simulation game where the player builds rockets for and launches little green characters into space. Within the game, there are multiple planets, moons, and asteroids the player can visit utilizing their own creations.[Wikipedia].

KSP is one of my most-played video games, with many hours sunk into it. Fortunately, KSP is build on the Unity Game Engine, which is a powerful and flexiable game engine that allows easy modifications of the game. So I decided to combine two of my hobbies and make mods for KSP!

Below are some of the mods I've made over the years for Kerbal Space Program.
